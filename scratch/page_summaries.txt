 Helping Families Grow with Care and Expertise Explore my Services Request an Appointment Growing up in a household where my father, a dedicated Gynaecologist, transformed part of our home into a compassionate clinic, I witnessed firsthand the transformative power of healthcare. Watching patients place their trust in him and leave with renewed hope left an indelible mark on me. From a young age, I knew I didn’t just want to become a doctor—I wanted to specialize in Gynaecology and make a meaningful difference in people's lives. 1989-1996 Laying the Foundations of a Medical Dream My journey began at Calcutta National Medical College, where I earned my MBBS and was awarded three accolades for my achievements in Anatomy. Following my father’s sage advice to gain practical experience, I immersed myself in various clinical roles, refining my skills before pursuing specialization. Although my initial plan was to study further in the UK, fate had other ideas. I found myself at Kasturba Medical College, Manipal, under the transformative mentorship of Dr. Pratap Kumar. Those rigorous years of intense training challenged me but also shaped my resilience and expertise, laying the groundwork for my future specialization. 1989-1996 2001-2004 A Passion for Fertility Management While at Manipal, my passion for Fertility Management was ignited. After earning my MD and securing a top rank, I briefly joined my father’s practice but soon realized my heart was set on helping couples struggling with infertility. I returned to Manipal to deepen my expertise. My journey took a significant leap under the mentorship of Dr. Sadhana Desai, a pioneer in IVF. Her meticulous approach, honesty, and dedication to quality control became guiding principles in my practice. Additionally, my time as an Observer at Cardiff Assisted Reproduction Centre in Wales, UK, provided me with valuable global perspectives that continue to inform my work today. 2001-2004 2005-2021 Expanding Access to Fertility Care Along with 2 other partners, I set up Care IVF. From there for 16 long years I set myself up for refining my skills in Fertility and Laparoscopy, providing affordable fertility treatment to the masses and setting up standard operating procedures so that the care and compassion is the core behind the treatments we provide. I set up an NGO called Hope Foundation through which I offered subsidised and free treatment to the economically weaker sections of society. I also set up International Fertility Academy, the first online platform in the world for training budding Gynaecologists in the field of Infertility management. In 2021, i decided to move on and set up Renew Healthcare. 2005-2021 2021 onwards My Awards &amp; Recognition Empowering Parenthood at Renew Healthcare Today, as the Medical Director of Renew Healthcare, I lead a dedicated and compassionate team focused on helping individuals and couples realize their dream of parenthood.
